Enjoy this beautiful Florida island

×

The Anna Maria City Pier, one of the island's main attractions. Another way to enjoy the island is to take the tram that runs all around the site, stopping at popular spots along the way. It also offers different types of activities for families: rent kayaks, paddle boards and bicycles to explore the mangrove tunnels or nature reserves.

Anna Maria Island is located on the coast of Manatee County in Florida, surrounded by the Intracoastal Waterway, Tampa Bay and the Gulf of Mexico. It has been described as a “piece of paradise” because of the blue color of its beaches. It is a strip of just over 11 kilometers of beautiful white sand beaches and turquoise waters. This destination is perfect for families due to its many activities for children, but also for spending a few days relaxing. The island is made up of three cities: Anna Maria City, Holmes Beach and Bradenton Beach. All unique communities blend effortlessly to create a friendly and welcoming atmosphere. Anna Maria Island can be explored by bike or on foot to enjoy the sunset.
